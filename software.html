SABE Ltd, provide traing in following enineering softwares:



  • ETABS

    ETABS (Extended Three-dimensional Analysis of Building Systems) is a comprehensive software package developed by Computers and Structures, Inc. (CSI) for the structural analysis and design of buildings. It is widely used by civil and structural engineers due to its powerful capabilities in modeling, analyzing, and designing various types of building structures.

  • AutoCAD

    AutoCAD is a widely-used software application developed by Autodesk for computer-aided design (CAD) and drafting. It is extensively utilized in various industries such as architecture, engineering, construction, manufacturing, and product design.

  • Autodest Revit

    Autodesk Revit is a comprehensive Building Information Modeling (BIM) software developed by Autodesk. It is used by architects, engineers, designers, and contractors to design, document, visualize, and manage building projects. Revit provides tools to create intelligent 3D models that encompass both the physical and functional characteristics of a building.

  • SketchUp

    SketchUp is a popular 3D modeling software developed by Trimble Inc. It's widely used in various industries, including architecture, interior design, landscape architecture, engineering, film, and video game design, due to its ease of use and versatility.

  • Civil 3D

    Autodesk Civil 3D is a design and documentation software application used by civil engineering professionals to plan, design, and manage civil infrastructure projects. It is part of the Autodesk family of software products and provides a comprehensive suite of tools for civil engineers, drafters, designers, and surveyors.

  • ARCHCAD

    ArchiCAD is a BIM (Building Information Modeling) software developed by Graphisoft for architects. It provides tools for designing, visualizing,
    and documenting building projects. Known for its robust 3D modeling capabilities, ArchiCAD facilitates collaboration and efficiency in architectural workflows.

  • Prokon

    Prokon is a comprehensive structural analysis and design software package developed by Prokon Software Consultants. It is widely used by civil and structural engineers for the analysis, design, and detailing of various types of structures, including buildings, bridges, towers, and industrial structures.

  • Adobe Illustrator

    Adobe Illustrator is a vector graphics software developed by Adobe Inc. It is widely used for creating illustrations, logos, icons, and other scalable graphic designs. Illustrator offers precision drawing tools and advanced features for professional graphic design and digital art.

  • Lumion

    Lumion is a 3D rendering software designed for architects and designers to create stunning visualizations of architectural projects. It allows for quick and easy rendering of images, videos, and panoramic views with real-time feedback. Lumion is known for its user-friendly interface and extensive library of materials and effects.

  • Adobe Photoshop

    Adobe Photoshop is a powerful image editing and graphic design software developed by Adobe Inc. It is widely used for photo retouching, digital art, and creating complex graphics. Photoshop offers a wide range of tools and features for manipulating images and designing various visual content.

  • Autodesk Robot

    Autodesk Robot Structural Analysis is a software application used for structural analysis and design. It enables engineers to simulate and analyze the behavior of structures under various loads and conditions. The software supports a wide range of materials and building codes, facilitating detailed and accurate engineering assessments.

  • Tekla Structures

    Tekla Structures is a BIM (Building Information Modeling) software developed by Trimble for structural engineering and construction. It enables the creation, analysis, and detailing of 3D models of steel and concrete structures. The software supports collaboration and enhances precision in the design, fabrication, and construction processes.

  • Twinmotion

    Twinmotion is a real-time 3D visualization software developed by Epic Games. It allows architects, designers, and urban planners to create high-quality images, panoramas, and virtual reality (VR) experiences from 3D models. The software is known for its user-friendly interface and integration with various design tools, enabling quick and immersive visualizations.
